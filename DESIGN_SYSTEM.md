# Sanatan App - Design System

## Design Philosophy

**Minimalistic, Homely, and Sanatani**

This app embodies the essence of Sanatan Dharma through:
- **Simplicity**: Clean, uncluttered interface that doesn't distract from the sacred content
- **Warmth**: Homely feeling with warm, earthy colors that evoke comfort and spirituality
- **Respect**: Design that honors the timeless wisdom being presented
- **Focus**: Every element serves the purpose of making shlokas accessible and meaningful

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Saffron Gold**: `#FF8C42` - Warm, spiritual, represents purity and knowledge
- **Deep Maroon**: `#8B2E3D` - Sacred, traditional, represents devotion
- **Cream White**: `#FFF8F0` - Soft, peaceful background
- **Warm Brown**: `#5C4A3A` - Earthy, grounding text color

### Background Colors
- **Primary Background**: `#FFF8F0` - Warm cream, easy on the eyes
- **Card Background**: `#FFFFFF` - Pure white for cards
- **Dark Mode Background**: `#2A1F1A` - Warm dark brown (not harsh black)

### Accent Colors
- **Saffron Light**: `#FFB366` - Subtle highlights
- **Maroon Light**: `#A64D5C` - Secondary accents
- **Gold**: `#D4AF37` - Special emphasis

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `#2A1F1A` - Warm dark brown (readable, not harsh)
- **Secondary Text**: `#6B5B4F` - Medium brown for metadata
- **Sanskrit Text**: `#8B2E3D` - Deep maroon for sacred text
- **Light Text**: `#9B8A7F` - Subtle text on light backgrounds

## Typography

### Font Hierarchy
- **Sanskrit/Devanagari**: Larger, prominent (24-28px) - the sacred text deserves prominence
- **Transliteration**: Medium (18-20px) - helps pronunciation
- **Translation/Explanation**: Body text (16-18px) - comfortable reading
- **Metadata**: Small (12-14px) - book, chapter, verse info

### Font Weights
- **Sanskrit**: Regular (400) - clean and readable
- **Headings**: Semi-bold (600) - clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Regular (400) - comfortable reading
- **Metadata**: Medium (500) - subtle emphasis

## Spacing System

- **Card Padding**: 32px horizontal, 40px vertical
- **Section Spacing**: 24px between major sections
- **Element Spacing**: 16px between related elements
- **Tight Spacing**: 8px for closely related items

## Card Design

### Structure
1. **Top Section**: Book name and reference (subtle, top)
2. **Sanskrit Text**: Centered, prominent, in Devanagari script
3. **Transliteration**: Below Sanskrit, italic, medium size
4. **Divider**: Subtle decorative element (Om symbol or lotus)
5. **Translation/Explanation**: Clear, readable body text
6. **Bottom**: Minimal progress indicator

### Visual Elements
- **Subtle Shadow**: Soft elevation (not harsh)
- **Rounded Corners**: 16px radius for warmth
- **Decorative Elements**: Minimal use of Om (‡•ê) or lotus motifs
- **No Images**: Focus on text, unless contextually relevant

## Interaction Design

### Swipe Gestures
- **Smooth Animations**: Gentle, respectful transitions
- **Visual Feedback**: Subtle rotation and fade
- **Haptic Feedback**: Soft vibration on swipe (optional)

### Loading States
- **Spinner**: Saffron gold color
- **Message**: "Loading divine wisdom..." (respectful language)

### Empty States
- **Message**: "No shlokas available. Pull to refresh."
- **Icon**: Subtle Om symbol

## Principles

1. **Content First**: Design never overshadows the sacred text
2. **Respectful**: Every design choice honors the spiritual nature
3. **Accessible**: Large text, good contrast, easy to read
4. **Calming**: Colors and spacing create a peaceful experience
5. **Timeless**: Design that feels eternal, not trendy
